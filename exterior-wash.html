Exterior Wash

Our exterior wash service will make your car shine like new. We use high-quality products to ensure the best results. Our process includes:

  • Pre-wash rinse to remove loose dirt and debris.
  • Application of high-quality car wash soap and foam.
  • Hand wash using microfiber mitts to avoid scratches.
  • Rinse and dry with soft, lint-free towels.
  • Cleaning and shining of tires and wheels.

Our professional team ensures that your car gets the best treatment, leaving it looking pristine and shiny. Try our exterior wash service today and experience the difference!
